ABOUT JEWELRY

History of Jewelry

The history of jewelry dates back to prehistoric times, starting with beads made from shells, bones, and teeth. These objects became symbols of wealth, power, and religious beliefs in ancient times, especially in the Egyptian and Mesopotamian civilizations. With the passage of time and the discovery of precious metals, the technology and creativity in jewelry making became more sophisticated, and different styles, such as floral and animal designs, emerged in periods such as the Baroque and Rococo.

ITALIAN JEWELRY

Italian jewelry is renowned for its high quality, elegant and modern designs, and durability,
and is generally known for the quality of its materials and the skill of its goldsmiths.
